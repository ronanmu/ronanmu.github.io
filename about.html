Hi!

My name is Ronan and I'm IT architect & consultant by trade, living & working in Dublin, Ireland. I've worked in various industries in my career to date, from local & central government, financial services including general & life insurance, retail banking & pensions, and aviation and marketing tech, across Ireland, the UK, Europe, North America & the the Middle East.

While I've worked with lots of different technology stacks and platforms, I'm most at home with Java/JEE and a big fan of Spring, which forms the basis of the Booker project. I've been using Spring since version 1.2.6 (from 2006 ) and have had the opportunity to use most projects from the Spring portfolio in my professional career. I even tried Spring.NET, anyone remember that?
